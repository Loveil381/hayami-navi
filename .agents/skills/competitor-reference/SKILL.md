# Competitor Intelligence

## Top Competitors

### 1. benri.jp/calc/nenrei (★ Best UX)
- Shows 満年齢 AND 数え年 side by side
- Marks 厄年 (男/女), 大厄, 長寿祝い (還暦/古希/喜寿/傘寿/米寿/卒寿/白寿/百寿)
- Marks school entry years (小1/中1/高1/大1)
- Cross-links to 入学卒業 calculator, 七五三 calculator
- ★ LEARN: Information density per table row

### 2. nenrei-hayami.net (★ Best SEO)
- Separate page per birth year (/1990.html, /1991.html, etc.)
- PDF download (A4 printable)
- 干支入り PDF version
- Very old but extremely high domain authority
- ★ LEARN: Long-tail SEO strategy with per-year pages

### 3. nenreis.com (★ Best Download)
- Excel AND PDF free download
- Shows both eras for boundary years (昭和64年/平成元年)
- Clean table design
- ★ LEARN: Download feature, dual era display

### 4. keisan.casio.jp (★ Best Brand Trust)
- CASIO brand, very functional calculator
- No SEO articles — pure tool
- ★ LEARN: Calculator precision and edge case handling
